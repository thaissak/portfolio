New York, US

November, 2011

This trip was the first international destination for us as couple and also the first time we both set foot on American soil (or concrete). We couldn't be more excited! Although we got exhausted from our 9 hour flight, we quickly recovered and found the energy we needed to go meet one of the most amazing cities in the world. After 5 days, we didn't want to leave and were often talking about how amazing would be to live in a city like that...great way to start our internationals getaways!


Highlight:

Hard to choose one thing but the view from the Empire State building was definitely one of the most amazing experiences, you feel like a little bug in the middle of the concrete jungle. Scary beautiful.


Low point:

Everything is crowded. I know, is part of the NY experience, but it was really annoying at some points.
