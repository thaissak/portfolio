Beyond work

Some people find that hard to believe, because of my asian looks (from my japanese grandparents on my dad's side and my chinese great-grandfather on my mom's side) but that's actually quite common in Brazil, we have one of the largest Japanese colonies outside Japan and a big Chinese community as well. I moved to New York in 2014 and lived there for 1 year, an amazing experience but the sunny and beautiful Bay Area is where I call home now!
