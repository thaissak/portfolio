Task Slaughter was born in an attempt to help organize people's days, keeping track of all the tasks they have throughout the day and showing them on Google Maps. The coolest feature is the ability to text yourself your task, so you can have the information you need ready to go on your phone. This group project was part of a Hackathon, being built and deployed in 4 days and won 1st place! Amazing!
